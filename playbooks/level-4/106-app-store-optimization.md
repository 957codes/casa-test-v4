# App Store Optimization and Mobile Growth

For a consumer app, the store listing is the front door and the install-to-activation path
is where most growth is won or lost. Recurring, because the stores and competitors move.

## Procedure

1. Research the keywords your audience searches, and the terms competitors rank for.
2. Optimize the listing: title, subtitle, keyword field, icon, screenshots, and the first
   few seconds of the preview. The icon and first screenshots drive most of the tap-through.
3. Tie the listing promise to the onboarding so the install actually activates; store
   ranking rewards retained installs, not just downloads.
4. Set up store ratings prompts at the right moment and a path for unhappy users to give
   feedback privately.
5. Instrument the funnel: impression to install to activation, and iterate the listing on
   the weakest step.

## Output

An `aso_plan`: the target keywords, the optimized listing assets, the activation tie-in,
and the funnel instrumentation, refreshed on a cadence.

## Rules

- Optimize organic before paying for installs; a leaky listing wastes paid spend.
- Retained installs, not raw downloads, are what the stores reward.
