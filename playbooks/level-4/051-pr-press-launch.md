# PR & Press Launch

The hire-an-agency-and-pray model is broken. This is a Go-Direct framework: owned
media (founder post, Substack, threads) is the canonical version of the story;
earned media is amplification, not the primary vehicle.

## Procedure

1. Finalize messaging against RIBS (Relevant, Inevitable, Believable, Simple) before
   contacting any journalist.
2. Draft and approve the owned-media asset first. It is the canonical story.
3. T-30: build a targeted media list and study each journalist's recent work.
4. Pitch earned media personalized and newsworthy. Every pitch shows you read the
   journalist's work. Never send an identical pitch to more than three journalists.
5. Sequence owned publish, then earned outreach and embargo or exclusive offers.
6. Treat paid media (wire distribution) as near-zero ROI pre-PMF; defer to Series A.

## Output

`press_coverage`: the approved owned-media asset, the media list, the pitch log, and
the resulting coverage, written to the company brain. Unblocks podcast tour and
community amplification.

## Rules

- Human gate on every public statement; published claims are hard to retract and
  carry legal and reputational risk (escalate per HITL gates).
- No mass-identical pitches; respect the journalist's finite credibility.
- No wire-distribution spend pre-PMF.

Full RIBS detail, channel mechanics, and outreach templates in the source draft
above. Condense, do not pad.
