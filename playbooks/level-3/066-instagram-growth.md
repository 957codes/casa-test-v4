# Instagram Growth

A Reels-first acquisition engine paired with DM-automation funnels, Story cadence,
and a tuned Highlight architecture. The algorithm now weights Sends and Saves
over Likes, and the link-in-bio is a low-conversion dead end.

## Procedure

1. Confirm fit and inputs. B2C audience present; ICP doc, brand voice, catalog with
   conversion URLs, asset library, and platform access (Instagram Business,
   ManyChat or equivalent, scheduler).
2. Optimize the profile. Bio, Highlights, and search-discoverable copy aligned to
   the ICP.
3. Produce Reels for discovery. Optimize for watch time, Sends, and Replays to
   reach non-followers.
4. Run the surface mix. Reels for acquisition, Feed for relationship, Stories for
   daily engagement and conversion.
5. Capture leads via DM funnel. Trigger automated DM flows from content CTAs; drive
   email opt-in inside the inbox, not via link-in-bio.
6. Measure and iterate. Watch non-follower reach, Saves, Sends, and DM opt-in rate.

## Cadence

Operate continuously: daily Stories, regular Reels; weekly analytics review.

## Output

`instagram_channel` and `short_form_video`, with funnel metrics in the company
brain.

## Rules

- B2C-leaning channel; do not force it on a pure B2B motion.
- DM funnels over link-in-bio for conversion.

Reels here are repurposable from TikTok winners (063).
