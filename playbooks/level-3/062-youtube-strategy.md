# YouTube Strategy

YouTube is a search-and-recommendation engine whose outputs compound. The
algorithm rewards relevance and satisfaction, not recency. Quality compounds;
volume decays.

## Procedure

1. Select the niche. Validate demand and a defensible angle; study 3-5 reference
   channels.
2. Engineer packaging. Thumbnail and title are the first product experience; they
   set whether the idea's ceiling is reached. Run the CCN check (Core, Casual, New)
   before producing.
3. Architect the hook. Earn the first 30 seconds; promise and deliver fast.
4. Script for retention. Pacing and structure are algorithmic inputs, not aesthetic
   choices; target high average view duration.
5. Produce and edit for retention. Cut dead air; reward attention.
6. Read analytics and iterate. Optimize CTR and retention per video; double down on
   formats that compound.

## Cadence

Operate continuously on a sustainable upload cadence; weekly analytics review.

## Output

`youtube_channel` and a library of `video_content`, with performance logged in the
company brain.

## Rules

- Make the best YouTube video, not the best video: structured for click-through and
  retention.
- One exceptional video beats ten mediocre ones; never trade quality for volume.
