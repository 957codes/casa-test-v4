# Design Partner Program

## Procedure
1. Pull the top 12 to 15 accounts from your ICP target list that have both the acute problem and budget authority. Rank by pain intensity and reachability, then assign a named champion for each.
2. Draft the partner offer: discounted paid pricing in exchange for deep access, a fixed feedback cadence, and the right to co-shape the roadmap. Make the dollar commitment explicit. A free pilot signals nothing, so require payment even if discounted.
3. Run direct outreach to all 12 to 15 within one week. Open with the specific problem you heard in validation interviews, not the product. Book 30 minute calls and walk each champion through the co-build offer and what they get for committing early.
4. Write the design partner agreement: scope, access expectations, weekly or biweekly feedback cadence, paid terms, success criteria with dates, and reference rights. Keep it to two pages so it signs fast.
5. Close 3 to 7 signatures. Aim higher in your funnel since half will stall. Each signed partner must name a budget owner and a first success milestone.
6. Set up a shared roadmap and a recurring feedback ritual so partners see their input shipped. This retention loop is what converts them into reference logos and renewals.

## Output
A signed design_partner_agreement with 3 or more paying partners and a co_build_roadmap they actively shape.
