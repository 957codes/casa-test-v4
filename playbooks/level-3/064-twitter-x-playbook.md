# Twitter / X Content Engine

A full-stack X engine: account foundation, content production, algorithmic
optimization, audience conversion, and risk management. Replace judgment calls
with explicit conditional logic.

## Procedure

1. Set the foundation. Optimize the profile around the ICP and conversion goal;
   confirm Premium status for reach and long-form.
2. Establish cadence. A sustainable daily posting rhythm of original posts plus
   threads.
3. Architect threads. Hook-first; one idea per thread; payoff that earns the read.
4. Work the reply surface. Engage adjacent and competitor audiences daily; the
   reply surface is the primary discovery engine.
5. Convert via DM. Move warm engagement into a DM flow toward the conversion goal
   (signup, demo, trial).
6. Measure and iterate. Track follower growth and engagement; double down on
   formats that work.

## Cadence

Operate continuously: daily posting and replies, weekly analytics-driven iteration.

## Output

`twitter_channel` and a growing `social_audience`, logged in the company brain.

## Rules

- The reply surface, not broadcast posting, drives early discovery.
- Repurpose `long_form_content` into threads; do not let posts decay unused.
