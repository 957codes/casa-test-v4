# Pre-seed Fundraise Narrative

The early raise sells the team, the insight, and the why-now, not metrics you do not have
yet. This builds what a pre-seed founder actually needs.

## Procedure

1. Sharpen the narrative: the problem, the non-obvious insight, why this team, and why now.
   At pre-seed this is the product.
2. Build the minimal deck (around ten slides): problem, insight, what you are building,
   why-now, team, market, and the ask. No five-year model.
3. Decide the round shape: amount, instrument (SAFE is typical), and the milestone the
   money buys.
4. Build the target investor list, ranked by fit and warm-path, and the intro plan.
5. Prepare the data room basics and the FAQ for the obvious objections.
6. Do not file, sign, or send terms without founder approval.

## Output

A `preseed_raise_plan`: the narrative, the pre-seed deck, the round shape and ask, the
ranked investor list with intro paths, and the objection FAQ.

## Rules

- Pre-seed sells the team and the insight; do not gate the raise on revenue or PMF.
- Never sign or send terms autonomously. Raising money is a human-gate, founder decision.
