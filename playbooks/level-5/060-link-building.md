# Link Building

Backlinks from high-authority, topically relevant domains are votes of confidence
that feed Google's domain-level quality signals and raise the odds of being cited
by AI answer engines. Link building is inseparable from brand building.

## Procedure

1. Confirm a linkable asset. Outreach only points at a live, indexable asset
   (`long_form_content`, original data, a tool). No asset, no campaign.
2. Score link quality. Qualify prospects on authority, topical relevance, and
   traffic before pitching.
3. Run channels. Digital PR and newsjacking, expert source pitching (HARO
   successors), resource-page outreach, broken-link building, and selective guest
   posting.
4. Execute outreach. Prospect, qualify, draft personalized pitches, send, track
   replies, and log every result in the CRM.
5. Reclaim and defend. Convert unlinked brand mentions; monitor for lost links.

## Cadence

Quarterly campaigns plus continuous triggers (new asset, ranking plateau,
competitor gap, link loss).

## Output

`backlinks` logged in the CRM and rising `domain_authority`, tracked in the
company brain.

## Rules

- Never run link building before a linkable asset exists; it wastes outreach
  domain reputation.
- Ethical channels only. No paid link schemes or PBNs.
- Relevance and authority over raw count.
